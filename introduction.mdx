---
title: Introduction
description: What is Defer?
---

Defer is a cloud platform for developers, allowing the creation, deployment and
management of background jobs on NodeJS. Queue, schedule, compose background
jobs. Avoid timeouts, improve your user experience and performance with no
infrastructure work.

Focus on your code: Defer is Framework agnostic and is compatible from Express
to Next.js, and with monorepos.

**Get started in under 5 minutes** and scale without any infrastructure work.
